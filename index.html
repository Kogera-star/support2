楽々ショッピング
(Comfort Shopping)

【お買い物先で使えるアプリ】

買い物に出掛けて買い忘れたことはありませんか？

直前までは覚えていたのにとがっかりすることはよくあります。

このアプリは、スマホを片手に買い物先で手軽に使えるアプリです。

Have you ever been disappointed that you forgot to buy something at the store?

It often happens that I remembered it before I went out.

This app is an app that can be used easily at shopping destinations with a smartphone in one hand.

As long as people handle personal information, there is a possibility of leakage.
